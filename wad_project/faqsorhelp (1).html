Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the Grievance Redressal System?

The Grievance Redressal System is a platform that allows individuals to submit complaints or grievances regarding various issues and seek timely resolutions.

2. How do I submit a grievance?

You can submit a grievance by visiting our Contact Us page and filling out the grievance form. Make sure to provide all necessary details for a prompt response.

3. How long does it take to resolve a grievance?

The time taken to resolve a grievance varies based on the complexity of the issue. However, we strive to address all grievances within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Can I track the status of my grievance?

Yes, once you submit your grievance, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to check the status of your grievance.

5. Whom do I contact for further assistance?

For further assistance, please contact our support team through the Contact Us page.
